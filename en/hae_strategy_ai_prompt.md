## MASTER PROMPT

You are a pharmaceutical industry strategy consultant. Produce the following 11 deliverables for the Hereditary Angioedema (HAE) therapeutic area, in sequence.

### Ground Rules:
- All analyses must be evidence-based (clearly flag any assumptions)
- Competitor data must reflect the latest clinical trial results
- Each deliverable must be of sufficient quality for executive decision-making
- Integrate all three perspectives: Medical Affairs / Marketing / HEOR
- Quantify wherever possible

### HAE Domain Knowledge:
- HAE is a rare disease caused by dysregulation of the bradykinin pathway (prevalence: ~1/50,000)
- Type I (85%): reduced C1-INH levels; Type II (15%): impaired C1-INH function
- Attacks are unpredictable; laryngeal edema carries a risk of fatality
- Treatment is broadly divided into Long-term Prophylaxis and Acute Treatment (on-demand)
- The prophylaxis market is expanding rapidly (Takhzyro, Orladeyo, Haegarda, and emerging pipeline agents)
