## 11 Deliverables

1. Executive Summary
2. U.S. Strategy Flow
3. International Strategy (INTL) Flow
4. U.S. vs INTL SWOT
5. Above-Market Integration
6. X1 (Strategy) → X2 (Tactics) → Y (Evidence Generation) → Z (Publication)
7. Vulnerability Analysis
8. KPIs
9. IEGP Strategic Materials
10. FY Summary
11. KPI/KGI Alignment Diagram

## Framework Structure

### Part 1: Design Philosophy
- Why these 11 deliverables
- Dependency sequencing and rationale
- 3-function integration principles (Medical Affairs / Marketing / HEOR)

### Part 2: Methodology per Deliverable
Each deliverable documented with 5 elements:
- **Why** — What decision does this support?
- **Perspective** — From what angle do we analyze?
- **How** — Step-by-step procedure (disease-agnostic)
- **Data Sources** — Where to find the information
- **Quality Gate** — How to judge completeness

### Part 3: Application Guide
Customization guidance for:
- Rare disease vs large market
- Oncology vs chronic disease
- First-in-class vs established market
- Biologics vs small molecules

## How to Use

### Manual (for humans)
Follow the step-by-step methodology in the manual. Adapt Part 3 for your specific therapeutic area.

### AI Prompt (for Claude/GPT/Gemini)
1. Open the AI prompt file
2. Fill in `[INPUT_REQUIRED]` fields with your product/disease information
3. Input the entire prompt to your AI assistant
4. The AI generates all 11 deliverables sequentially
